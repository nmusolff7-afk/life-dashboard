Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Apex Leadership LLC collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use Life Dashboard at lifedashboardapp.com and related services.

Who we are

Life Dashboard is operated by Apex Leadership LLC. If you have privacy questions, you can contact us at nathan@lifedashboardapp.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as username, email address, and authentication data.
  • Health, fitness, and lifestyle information you choose to enter, including meals, workouts, sleep, check-ins, and goals.
  • Connected service data if you authorize integrations such as Garmin or Gmail.
  • Technical information such as device, browser, IP address, and basic usage logs needed to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Life Dashboard.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Generate product features, analytics, summaries, and AI-assisted outputs you request.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate service updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

AI and service providers

Some features use third-party service providers to process information on our behalf, including infrastructure, analytics, email, and AI providers. We use these providers to deliver the service and do not sell your personal information.

Data sharing

We may share information with service providers, professional advisors, or legal authorities when reasonably necessary to operate the service, protect users, comply with law, or complete a business transfer. We do not share your personal information for third-party advertising sales.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain business records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. You may request account deletion, and we will process eligible deletion requests subject to legal or operational retention requirements.

Your choices

  • You may update or delete certain information inside the product.
  • You may disconnect optional integrations.
  • You may contact us to request account deletion or ask privacy questions.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Life Dashboard is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version here and revise the effective date.
